Welcome to Cane Exchange:

Your resource for locating, donating, and learning about assistive devices that can help you get around.

Click on an option below to get started:

  • Dashboard: Shows devices that you've saved, devices that you're offering for donation, messages from others.
  • Find a Device: Lets you search for devices in your zip code that are being donated.
  • Donate a Device: Lets you put in your device details to offer it for donation to another user on this site.
  • Learn About Devices: Gives you information about who can safely use each device and links to pages about how to use the and where to buy them.
